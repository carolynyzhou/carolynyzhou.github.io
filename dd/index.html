Art Lessons:
Drawing & Design

Welcome!

I offer attentive guidance in teaching a range of art techniques, from fundamentals in drawing to logo design.

Who Am I?

A lifelong art student and current UX designer in tech, currently based in Palo Alto, CA. My experience ranges from traditional art media (i.e. pencil sketching, painting) to digital design tools (i.e. Photoshop, Sketch). I desire to give back and pass on the techniques and values I’ve learned.

Classes

I’ve been giving virtual lessons since the start of social distancing, adapting quickly to students’ needs and circumstances. Contact me to discuss how I can meet your art learning needs!

Distance Learning —

Classes typically may involve a "teaching" portion where I demonstrate technique or how to set up a drawing (screenshare setup below), followed by guided working time for the students. Students periodically show their progress on video or sending a photo for verbal feedback.

At the moment, I am only accepting students middle school and up.

Method/Approach —

I begin with the student's learning goals in art. Younger students may start with exploring a range of basic drawing skills, while older students may indicate more specific interests in traditional media, digital art or design.

Depending on the student's artistic goals and schedule, I may place them with a small group of other students or hold private lessons.
